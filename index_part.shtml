Corporation Background

Founded in 2002, K-Supplier., Ltd. researches, manufactures, develops and sources a wide range of quality chemical compounds, primarily:
aromatic organic intermediates
intermediates for pesticides
intermediates for pharmaceuticals
active pharmaceutical ingredients (APIs)

Reliable
K-Supplier mission is to become a reliable world supplier in fine chemicals and custom manufacturing. Our value-added benefits to customers include: prompt, reliable and thorough responses before, during and after order receipt; competitive pricing; timely delivery; and attentive after-sales-services. With international standards in sales and global concepts in minds, K-Supplier’ well trained staffs guarantee excellence in service.

Efficient Purchasing System

The marketing professionals at K-Supplier monitor the situation of Chinese manufacturers in the chemicals, pharmaceuticals, and agrochemicals industries. K-Supplier has gathered data on nearly 30,000 products from more than 3,000 manufacturers, and now has a complete, unique and constantly updated purchasing network.
